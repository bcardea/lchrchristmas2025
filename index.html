Load up the car, hit play on your favorite holiday playlist, and follow our curated tour of Chesapeake’s most dazzling Christmas lights.

© Lifepoint Church · Hampton Roads

The Lifepoint Christmas Light Tour

Starting Point: Lifepoint Church (600 Jarman Rd, Chesapeake, VA 23322)

Create a playlist, designate a navigator, and keep your radio handy for the music-synced shows along the way.

Part 1: The Western Branch Loop

Begin northwest toward Western Branch for a neighborhood bursting with cheer.

  • Stop 1: The Dock Landing Corner

    Address: 4107 Woodland Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23321

    Why visit: A beautifully decorated corner lot anchoring a festive neighborhood. Cruise nearby streets for bonus displays.

  • Stop 2: The Garden of Wonders

    Address: 4319 Topsail Landing, Chesapeake, VA 23321

    Why visit: Unique characters, flying reindeer, and an enormous abominable snowman fill this garden spectacle.

Part 2: The Deep Creek Spectacle

Head south to Deep Creek for two legendary synchronized light shows.

  • Stop 3: Matson Family Lights

    Address: 3316 Dietz Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23320

    Why visit: Around 35,000 lights dance to music. Tune to the posted FM station when you arrive.

  • Stop 4: The Waterside Spectacle

    Address: 1337 Simon Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23320

    Why visit: The shimmering water reflections make this the crown jewel of the tour.

Part 3: The Hickory Detour

Continue southeast toward Hickory for another music-synced display.

  • Stop 5: The Shore Road Show

    Address: 1109 Shore Road, Chesapeake, VA 23323

    Why visit: An expertly choreographed blend of light and sound. Tune your radio per the on-site sign.

☕ Intermission: Hot Chocolate Pit Stop

Warm up on your drive north toward Great Bridge.

  • Wawa

    Address: 101 Hillcrest Pkwy, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why stop: Quick in and out with hot chocolate (and coffee!) to keep the crew cozy.

Part 4: The Great Bridge Grand Finale

Close out the night near your starting point with five stops in Great Bridge.

  • Stop 6: A Frozen Wonderland

    Address: 801 Pin Oak Pl, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why visit: A family favorite bursting with Frozen characters.

  • Stop 7: Pine Level Lane's Classic Christmas

    Address: 909 Pine Level Ln, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why visit: A joyful mix of colorful lights and charming inflatables.

  • Stop 8: The Snowman Sanctuary

    Address: 910 Weeping Willow Dr, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why visit: An entire yard dedicated to snowmen—a must-see for Frosty fans.

  • Stop 9: Chattanooga Street's Elegant Glow

    Address: 919 Chattanooga St, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why visit: Tasteful white lights and displays among the trees create a serene scene.

  • Stop 10: North Haven Circle's Dynamic Display

    Address: 966 North Haven Circle, Chesapeake, VA 23322

    Why visit: A high-energy show synchronized to music. Roll down the windows and enjoy!

After the finale, you are only minutes from Lifepoint Church. Have a wonderful and very Merry Christmas!
