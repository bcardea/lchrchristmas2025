The Lifepoint Christmas Light Tour

From our Hampton Roads family to yours, here’s a festive driving adventure that begins and ends at Lifepoint Church.

Starting Point

Lifepoint Church
600 Jarman Rd, Chesapeake, VA 23322

  • Create a Christmas playlist for the drive.
  • Designate a navigator to share directions and fun facts.
  • Keep your car radio handy for synchronized shows.

Part 1: The Western Branch Loop

Stop 1 — The Dock Landing Corner

4107 Woodland Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23321

A beautifully decorated corner lot anchoring a neighborhood known for its festive spirit. Drive a few extra streets for hidden gems!

Stop 2 — The Garden of Wonders

4319 Topsail Landing, Chesapeake, VA 23321

Unique characters, flying reindeer, and even a giant abominable snowman fill this expansive garden display.

Part 2: The Deep Creek Spectacle

Stop 3 — Matson Family Lights

3316 Dietz Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23320

About 35,000 lights synchronized to music make this beloved local tradition a dazzling highlight. Tune your radio per the onsite sign.

Stop 4 — The Waterside Spectacle

1337 Simon Drive, Chesapeake, VA 23320

The reflection of thousands of lights shimmering on the water creates a breathtaking, can’t-miss moment.

Part 3: The Hickory Detour

Stop 5 — The Shore Road Show

1109 Shore Road, Chesapeake, VA 23323

A high-energy, music-synced display. Roll down the windows and follow the posted FM frequency for the full experience.

Intermission: Hot Chocolate Pit Stop

Wawa
101 Hillcrest Pkwy, Chesapeake, VA 23322

Warm up with hot chocolate or coffee before the grand finale cluster right around the corner.

Part 4: The Great Bridge Grand Finale

Stop 6 — A Frozen Wonderland

801 Pin Oak Pl, Chesapeake, VA 23322

Bring the kids to see Frozen favorites like Elsa, Anna, and Olaf shining bright.

Stop 7 — Pine Level Lane's Classic Christmas

909 Pine Level Ln, Chesapeake, VA 23322

Colorful lights and cheerful blow-up displays deliver classic Christmas charm.

Stop 8 — The Snowman Sanctuary

910 Weeping Willow Dr, Chesapeake, VA 23322

A cohesive snowman theme that delights Frosty fans of all ages.

Stop 9 — Chattanooga Street's Elegant Glow

919 Chattanooga St, Chesapeake, VA 23322

Serene, white lights cast an elegant glow among the trees for a peaceful scene.

Stop 10 — North Haven Circle's Dynamic Display

966 North Haven Circle, Chesapeake, VA 23322

Animated lights dance in sync with music played aloud—roll down the windows and enjoy!

Finish

In just a minute or two, you’ll arrive back at 600 Jarman Rd, ready to celebrate Christmas with Lifepoint.

Have a wonderful and very Merry Christmas!
